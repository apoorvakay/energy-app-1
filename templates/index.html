Worldwide Access to Energy per Year

The choropleth map shows the the percentage in each country of people who have access to electricity in any given year. You may use the slider to change the selected year between 1990 and 2014, and observe the transitions.




In the line chart, you will see the maximum, minimum and mean value for access to electrictiy (in percentage). Clicking on a country in the map above will show it's line on the line chart for comparison.
